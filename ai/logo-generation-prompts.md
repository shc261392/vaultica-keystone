## Core Concept: "Blink"

The logo must evoke:

- **Speed** — Lightning-fast, instant capture
- **Visual/Eye** — The act of blinking, seeing
- **Precision** — Clean, intentional, geometric
- **Security** — Vault-like solidity (subtle)

---

## Symbol Ideas for "Blink"

### 1. Eye Concepts

- **Minimalist eye** with a lightning bolt as the pupil
- **Abstract eye** formed by geometric shapes
- **Eye + frame** suggesting visual capture
- **Stylized eyelid** mid-blink with motion lines

### 2. Speed/Lightning Concepts

- **Lightning bolt** in negative space of a circular frame
- **Speed lines** forming a "B" lettermark
- **Flash/burst** pattern suggesting instant capture
- **Motion blur** effect on geometric shape

### 3. Vault/Lock Hybrid

- **Eye as a vault door** (keyhole as pupil)
- **Lock with lightning strike** motif
- **Shield with eye** integration

### 4. Abstract Lettermark

- **"B" with blink motion** — letter with dynamic elements
- **"BV" monogram** for Blink Vault
- **Speed-traced "B"** with trailing motion

## AI Prompt Templates

### Prompt 1: Minimalist Eye Symbol

```text
Design a minimalist logo icon for "Blink", a visual collection app.
The logo should be an abstract eye symbol with a lightning bolt integrated as the pupil or iris.
Style: geometric, industrial elegance, brutalist precision.
Colors: electric blue (#27abec) accent on dark charcoal (#363b42) background.
The eye should feel fast, precise, and secure. No rounded/bubbly shapes.
Vector-ready, single color works well. Clean lines, no gradients.
```

### Prompt 2: Speed "B" Lettermark

```text
Create a modern lettermark logo for "Blink" - a lightning-fast visual saving tool.
Based on the letter "B" with integrated speed/motion elements.
Style: bold, geometric, industrial. Heavy visual weight.
Primary color: bright electric blue (#27abec).
The design should feel swift, intentional, and professional.
Avoid playful or casual aesthetics. Think "vault security meets lightning speed".
Flat design, suitable for app icon and branding.
```

### Prompt 3: Blink Symbol (Abstract)

```text
Design an abstract symbol logo for "Blink Vault" - an app that captures visual inspiration instantly.
Concept: combine the ideas of "blinking" (eyes, instant) with "vault" (secure, solid).
Style: brutalist, geometric, industrial elegance. Heavy borders, clean shapes.
Color: electric blue (#27abec) on dark (#363b42) or with white (#f0fbff).
The logo should work at small sizes (app icon) and large (marketing).
No decorative elements. Function-first design.
```

### Prompt 4: Eye-Frame Capture

```text
Create a logo combining an eye and a frame/capture symbol for "Blink".
The concept is "you see it, you save it" - instant visual capture.
Style: minimal, geometric, bold. Industrial precision, not playful.
Colors: bright blue (#27abec) as primary. Works on dark and light backgrounds.
Should feel like a premium fintech or security app, not a fun consumer app.
Vector style, clean edges, suitable for SVG export.
```

### Prompt 5: Lightning Vault Lock

```text
Design a logo icon that combines a lightning bolt with a vault/lock motif.
For "Blink Vault" - speed meets security.
The lightning should feel integrated, not forced. Perhaps as the keyhole or lock mechanism.
Style: geometric, industrial, heavy visual weight.
Color: electric blue (#27abec) on charcoal (#363b42).
Should feel trustworthy, fast, and modern. No cartoonish elements.
```

## Style Requirements for AI

Always include in prompts:

- ✅ "Geometric, industrial elegance"
- ✅ "Bold, heavy visual weight"
- ✅ "Clean lines, no gradients"
- ✅ "Works at small sizes (app icon)"
- ✅ "Vector-ready, flat design"
- ✅ "Professional, not playful"

Never include:

- ❌ Rounded, bubbly, playful styles
- ❌ Gradients or complex effects
- ❌ 3D or skeuomorphic elements
- ❌ Decorative illustrations
- ❌ Handwritten or script fonts
